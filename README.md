# Talks-writing-and-learning
# [Product / Project Name]

## 1. Context
Short background, company, market, and constraints (regulated industry, multi-region, etc.).

## 2. Problem
What problem were we trying to solve? For whom? Why now?

## 3. My Role
What I owned (product strategy, roadmap, discovery, execution, stakeholder management, CS, etc.).

## 4. Approach
- Discovery & research
- Strategy & prioritization
- Design & technical alignment
- Delivery & governance

## 5. Solution
High-level overview of the product/feature.
- Key flows
- Architecture at a conceptual level
- Integrations and dependencies

## 6. Impact
- Business impact (revenue, ARR, TPV, NRR, productivity, latency)
- Customer impact (NPS/CSAT, churn, adoption)
- Org impact (alignment, governance, teams)

## 7. Learnings & Next Steps
What worked, what didn’t, and how I’d evolve it.
